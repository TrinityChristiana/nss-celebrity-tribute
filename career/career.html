Introduction

Lizzo is an American singer, rapper, and flutist who is known for her dynamic performances, vibrant style, and the empowering and socially conscious messages in her music. Her music spans pop, hip hop, R&B, soul, gospel, and funk-pop. Growing up, Lizzo was a member of her school's marching band, and she began rapping with friends at age 14. Later, she majored in classical flute performance at the University of Houston.

Skills & Talents

Lizzo is known for her talents as a rapper, singer, flutist, and actress.

Places of Residence

Lizzo was born in 1988 in Detroit, MI and moved to Houston, TX in 1997 at age 9. She moved to Minneapolis, MN in 2011 at the start of her career as a recording artist. Her rising stardom propelled her to move to Los Angeles, CA, in 2016, where she currently resides.
