Skills & Talents

Places of Residence

Lizzo was born in 1988 in Detroit, MI and moved to Houston, TX in 1997 at age 9. She moved to Minneapolis, MN in 2011 at the start of her career as a recording artist. Her rising stardom propelled her decision to move to Los Angeles, CA, in 2016, where she currently resides.
